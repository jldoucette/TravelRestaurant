Where Should I Eat?

This site allows you to search major US cities and search local restaurants by cuisine type, providing a list sorted by rating and displayed on a map. A link to the menu is provided and you can add the restaurants to a city list which is tied to your username and email and displayed upon login! The Top 10 Restaurants are displayed, based on the distance you are willing to travel.
